Frequently Asked Questions

What is moringa powder?

Moringa powder is made from dried moringa leaves, a nutrient-dense superfood native to India. Our powder is shade-dried (not sun-dried) to preserve nutrients.

How do I use moringa powder?

Add 1 teaspoon (3–5g) to smoothies, juice, yogurt, or meals daily. Start with ½ teaspoon for the first week, then increase.

Is moringa safe?

Yes. Moringa powder is generally recognized as safe. Pregnant or breastfeeding women and people on medication should consult their doctor first.

How much moringa per day?

Adults: 1–2 teaspoons (3–10g) daily. Start with ½ teaspoon and increase gradually. Don't exceed 3 teaspoons per day.

Does moringa have side effects?

Most people tolerate moringa well. Some may experience digestive upset if starting with too much. Start small, increase gradually. Read our side effects guide.

Why is your moringa fresher?

What's the difference between powder and capsules?

Powder is fresher, more potent, and better value. Capsules are convenient but often contain less moringa per serving. Read our full comparison.

Is moringa banned in Australia?

No. Moringa is completely legal in Australia. This myth comes from confusion with other supplements. Read the full truth.
