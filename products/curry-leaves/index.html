How to Use Dried Curry Leaves

Use 2-3 times the amount of dried leaves compared to fresh. Fry in hot oil or ghee at the start of cooking (tadka method) to release their aroma. Perfect for curries, dals, sambars, and South Indian dishes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do dried curry leaves compare to fresh?

Dried curry leaves are more convenient and have a longer shelf life. Use about 2-3 times the amount of dried leaves compared to fresh for the same flavor intensity.

What dishes can I use them in?

Perfect for curries, dals, sambars, rice dishes, and South Indian vegetable preparations. They add authentic flavor to any dish. Available for delivery throughout Australia.

How should I store them?

Keep in an airtight container away from heat and light. Stored properly, they maintain their flavor for 6+ months.
