How to Use Moringa Soap

+

Wet your skin and the soap bar. Lather the soap in your hands or directly on your body. Massage gently in circular motions to cleanse. Rinse thoroughly with water. For best results, use daily as part of your skincare routine.

Pro tip: Allow the soap to dry between uses by keeping it on a well-draining soap dish. This extends the life of your soap and maintains its quality.
