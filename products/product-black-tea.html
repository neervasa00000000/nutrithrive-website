What Makes Our Darjeeling Black Tea Special?

+

Our premium Darjeeling black tea is sourced directly from certified gardens in the Himalayan region, where the unique terroir and high altitude create the distinctive muscatel flavor that tea connoisseurs worldwide cherish. Known as the "champagne of teas," Darjeeling offers a delicate balance of floral, fruity, and slightly astringent notes that set it apart from other black teas. Each batch is carefully selected and processed to preserve the tea's natural antioxidants, which support heart health, circulation, and overall wellness. With moderate caffeine content (40-50mg per cup), it provides a gentle energy boost without the jitters associated with coffee. Whether enjoyed plain to appreciate its nuanced flavors or with a splash of milk and honey for a traditional breakfast tea, our Darjeeling black tea delivers an exceptional tea-drinking experience that elevates your daily routine.

How to Brew Darjeeling Black Tea

+

Use 1-2 teaspoons of loose tea per cup. Heat water to 85-90°C (not boiling). Steep for 3-5 minutes for optimal flavor. Enjoy plain or with a splash of milk and honey.
