Are curry leaves the same as curry powder?

No. Curry leaves come from the Murraya koenigii tree; curry powder is a spice blend.

Can I substitute dried for fresh?

Yes — use 2–3× more dried leaves and temper them early in hot oil for best flavour.

Where can I buy dried curry leaves near me in Melbourne?

Check local Indian grocery stores in Springvale, Dandenong, Footscray, or your nearest multicultural shopping area. Many suburban Indian stores stock dried curry leaves year-round.

Are dried curry leaves as good as fresh ones?

Dried curry leaves develop deeper, roasted notes when fried and have a much longer shelf life (6-12 months vs 3-7 days fresh). Use 2-3 times more dried leaves to match fresh intensity.

What's the best way to store dried curry leaves?

Keep them in an airtight container in a cool, dark pantry. Avoid exposure to light and moisture to maintain their aromatic properties and extend shelf life.
