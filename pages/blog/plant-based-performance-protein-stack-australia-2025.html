The Protein Stack Revolution (Why One Protein Isn’t Enough)

Single-source powders can leave gaps. A smart stack combines complementary proteins so you cover all essential amino acids, improve texture and digestion, and add micronutrients for daily wellness.

  • Pea + faba bean: high lysine, easy on digestion, low allergen.
  • Pumpkin seed + lupin: boosts magnesium, arginine and fibre for heart and gut health.
  • Chia + flax: omega‑3s and lignans to smooth energy and support hormones.
  • Moringa leaf: bioavailable protein with iron, calcium and polyphenols—your green advantage.

Gut Health & Recovery: The Winning Edge

Training results come faster with a supported microbiome. Fibre, greens and polyphenols help reduce digestive load, improve regularity and enhance nutrient absorption—so your protein actually works harder for you.

  • Increase fermented foods or a simple probiotic.
  • Include prebiotic seeds (chia/flax) for soluble fibre.
  • Add greens like moringa for polyphenols and minerals.

Coach tip: Blend almond milk, frozen berries, 1 scoop pea/faba, 1 tbsp chia, and 1 tsp NutriThrive Moringa. Optional: a cooled shot of adaptogenic black tea pre‑session.

Real-World Recipes Aussies Love

Protein Oats

Stir pea/lupin blend into hot oats. Top with chia, dried fruit and a drizzle of peanut butter. Finish with moringa for a green micronutrient boost.

Performance Bliss Balls

Pulse dates, almonds, cacao, chia, a scoop of protein and moringa. Roll and chill—no‑mess fuel.

Pre‑Workout Iced Tea

Brew black tea, chill, add lemon and ½ tsp moringa. Light lift, clean focus.
