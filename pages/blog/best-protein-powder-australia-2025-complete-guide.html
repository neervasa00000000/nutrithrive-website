‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How much protein powder should I take?

Most people need 1.2-2.2g of protein per kg of body weight daily. For a 70kg person, that's 84-154g total protein per day. Protein powder can provide 20-30g of this.

When is the best time to take protein powder?

Within 30 minutes after workouts for muscle building, or as a meal replacement for weight loss. Some people also take it before bed for overnight recovery.

Is protein powder safe for everyone?

Generally yes, but consult your doctor if you have kidney issues or are pregnant. Start with smaller doses and monitor your body's response.

What's the difference between whey and plant protein?

Whey is complete protein from dairy, while plant proteins may need combining (like rice + pea) for complete amino acid profiles. Both can be effective for muscle building.
