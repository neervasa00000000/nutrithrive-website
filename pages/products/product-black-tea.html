How to Brew Darjeeling Black Tea

+

Use 1-2 teaspoons of loose tea per cup. Heat water to 85-90°C (not boiling). Steep for 3-5 minutes for optimal flavor. Enjoy plain or with a splash of milk and honey.

Frequently Asked Questions

+

What makes Darjeeling tea special?

Darjeeling tea is grown at high altitude in the Himalayas, giving it a unique muscatel flavor and delicate aroma that's often called the "champagne of teas."

How much caffeine does it contain?

Darjeeling black tea contains moderate caffeine - about 40-50mg per cup, providing a gentle energy boost without jitters.

Can I drink it with milk?

Yes! Darjeeling tea pairs beautifully with milk and can be enjoyed as a traditional breakfast tea or afternoon treat.
