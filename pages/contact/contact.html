Common questions before you write

How fast do you ship? Orders placed before 2pm on business days usually leave our Truganina warehouse the same day. Melbourne metro often receives parcels in one to three business days; other states typically three to seven business days depending on carrier routes.

Do you sell wholesale? Yes — mention your business name, estimated monthly volume, and delivery state in the form below. We reply with wholesale pricing and minimum order details.

Is your moringa certified organic? We are not certified organic. We focus on small-batch shade-dried powder, batch codes you can trace, and third-party lab testing. See our privacy and product policy for full transparency.

Can I visit in person? We are a dispatch warehouse, not a retail shop. Pick-up may be arranged for large wholesale orders — email first so we can confirm a time.

Order or product help? Include your order number (if you have one) and the product name so we can check batch and tracking quickly.
