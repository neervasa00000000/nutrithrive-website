FAQs: Using Moringa Powder

Q: Can I cook with moringa powder?
Yes! Add to soups, stews, or baked goods. Avoid high heat for long periods to preserve nutrients.
Q: What does moringa taste like?
Mild, earthy, slightly green. Blends well with fruits, chocolate, and savory dishes.
Q: Is moringa safe for kids and seniors?
Yes, in small amounts. Start with 1/8 to 1/4 teaspoon and consult a healthcare provider if needed.
Q: How do I mask the taste of moringa?
Blend with banana, berries, or cocoa powder in smoothies, or mix into sauces and dressings.
Q: Can I take moringa every day?
Yes! Daily use is safe for most people. Start slow and increase as tolerated.
