Our story: why we started NutriThrive

Founded in 2020 and launched to customers in 2024, NutriThrive grew out of one frustration: most moringa on Australian shelves looked green online, but arrived dull—low aroma, flat taste, and often older stock.

We decided to do it differently: lab test shipments in Australia, focus on shade-drying, insist on 100% leaf-only powder, and publish what the lab measured so you are not guessing. For the longer personal version, read why we built NutriThrive.

Today we still run small batches, transparent sourcing, and the same obsession with fresh colour and fine texture—built for busy Australian routines without hype.

What makes our moringa different?

  • Lab-tested batches: heavy metals, pesticides and quality markers—with a published summary.
  • Leaf-only, no stems: higher chlorophyll and nutrient density per spoonful.
  • Shade-dried, not scorched: protects delicate phytonutrients and flavour.
  • Fine, soft texture: blends easily into smoothies, yoghurt and drinks.
  • Batch-dated: we ship soon after packing where possible so you can taste the difference.

How we process our moringa: four steps from farm to Truganina

  1. 01

    Grown with care

    Our moringa trees are grown in nutrient-rich soil on partner farms in India using traditional, sustainable practices.

  2. 02

    Hand-picked & shade-dried

    Only vibrant leaves are chosen, then gently shade-dried – never exposed to harsh heat that can dull colour and nutrients.

  3. 03

    Finely milled, never diluted

    Leaves are milled into a smooth powder with no fillers or additives – just 100% moringa leaf.

  4. 04

    Lab-tested & packed in Australia

    Every shipment is tested here, then packed in small runs at our Truganina warehouse and sent out with tracking.
