How we make it

Leaf from partner farms in India, shade-dried there to preserve colour, shipped to Truganina, tested in Australia, then packed in small runs and dispatched with tracking. Four steps, nothing added.

  1. 01

    Leaf from partner farms

    Moringa grows on partner farms in India. We pick leaves, shade-dry them there, then ship to Truganina for testing and packing.

  2. 02

    Hand-picked & shade-dried

    Leaves are shade-dried, not blasted with direct sun. Keeps the green colour.

  3. 03

    Finely milled, never diluted

    Leaves are milled into a smooth powder with no fillers or additives – just 100% moringa leaf.

  4. 04

    Lab-tested & packed in Australia

    Every shipment is tested here, then packed in small runs at our Truganina warehouse and sent out with tracking.
