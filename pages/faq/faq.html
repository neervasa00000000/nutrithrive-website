How much Moringa powder should I take daily?

+

Start with half a teaspoon daily for one week, then 1 teaspoon (about 2–3 g) once or twice daily if tolerated. Take with food. Dosage guide · Shop powder.

Any safety notes?

+

Leaf powder is widely eaten as food. Avoid if you are on blood thinners unless your GP agrees. Pregnant, breastfeeding, or on prescription medication: ask your healthcare provider first.

What are NutriThrive Dried Curry Leaves?

How do I use dried curry leaves?

+

Use 2-3 times the amount of dried leaves compared to fresh. Fry in hot oil or ghee at the start of cooking (tadka method) to release their aroma. Perfect for curries, dals, sambars, and South Indian dishes. Shop Dried Curry Leaves Here.

How should I store dried curry leaves?

+

Keep in an airtight container away from heat and light. Stored properly, they maintain their flavor for 6+ months. Store in a cool, dry place for best results.

How do I brew Darjeeling black tea?

+
