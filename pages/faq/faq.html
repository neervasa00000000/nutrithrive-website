Dried Curry Leaves

What are NutriThrive Dried Curry Leaves? expand_more

Premium dried curry leaves (karipatta) from our own farm. Perfect for curries, dals, and South Indian dishes. Packed in Melbourne.

Shop Curry Leaves →

How do I use dried curry leaves? expand_more

Use 2–3× the amount vs fresh. Fry in hot oil or ghee at the start (tadka) to release aroma—ideal for curries, dals, and sambar.

Shop Curry Leaves →

How should I store dried curry leaves? expand_more

Keep in an airtight container away from heat and light. Stored properly, flavour lasts 6+ months in a cool, dry place.

Darjeeling Black Tea

What is NutriThrive Darjeeling Black Tea? expand_more

Premium Darjeeling black tea—the "champagne of teas"—with muscatel, floral and fruity notes. Shipped from our Melbourne warehouse.

How do I brew Darjeeling black tea? expand_more

Use 1–2 tsp per cup. Heat water to 85–90°C (not boiling). Steep 3–5 minutes. Enjoy plain or with milk and honey.
