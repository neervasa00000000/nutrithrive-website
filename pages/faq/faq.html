What are NutriThrive Dried Curry Leaves?

+

Premium dried curry leaves (karipatta) sourced from traditional Indian suppliers. Perfect for authentic Indian cooking, adding depth and flavor to curries, dals, and South Indian dishes.

How do I use dried curry leaves?

+

Use 2-3 times the amount of dried leaves compared to fresh. Fry in hot oil or ghee at the start of cooking (tadka method) to release their aroma. Perfect for curries, dals, sambars, and South Indian dishes.

How should I store dried curry leaves?

+

Keep in an airtight container away from heat and light. Stored properly, they maintain their flavor for 6+ months. Store in a cool, dry place for best results.

What is NutriThrive Darjeeling Black Tea?

+

Premium Darjeeling black tea from the Himalayan region of India. Known as the "champagne of teas," it offers a unique muscatel flavor with floral and fruity notes.

How do I brew Darjeeling black tea?

+

Use 1-2 teaspoons of loose tea per cup. Heat water to 85-90°C (not boiling). Steep for 3-5 minutes for optimal flavor. Enjoy plain or with a splash of milk and honey.

How much caffeine does Darjeeling tea contain?

+

Darjeeling black tea contains moderate caffeine - about 40-50mg per cup, providing a gentle energy boost without jitters. Perfect for morning or afternoon enjoyment.
