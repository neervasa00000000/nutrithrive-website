FAQs

Will this work for cystic acne?

Yes — I had 15–20 active cysts. Expect weeks 1–4 slow (gut healing), 5–8 ~40–50% better, 9–12 ~70–80%, months 4–6 ~90–95%. Be patient.

Do I really need to cut dairy?

For most hormonal acne: yes. Cut 30 days strict, reintroduce 1 serving, watch 48–72 hours. If you break out, it's a trigger.

No. Sugar, dairy, and processed food undo probiotics. Remove triggers + add healers (probiotics, moringa, real food).

Weeks 2–3: digestion · 4–6: fewer new breakouts · 8–10: healing · 12–16: 70–80% clear · 20–24: 90–95%. Weeks 1–4 may "purge" — push through.
