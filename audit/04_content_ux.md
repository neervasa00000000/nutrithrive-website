# Phase 4 — Content and UX audit

**Site:** [https://nutrithrive.com.au](https://nutrithrive.com.au)  
**Audience:** Health-conscious Australian shoppers, culturally diverse (including Chinese-language moringa guide), Melbourne/local interest.

---

## Homepage

| Area | Assessment |
|------|------------|
| **H1 and value** | Hero communicates **“superfoods in Australia”** and product categories — clear **what** is sold. Strengthen **why NutriThrive** in one line (tested, Melbourne dispatch) next to the H1 if not already prominent in first screen. |
| **Above the fold** | Value props, product entry points, and trust (reviews) appear — **good**. Review **carousel** in markdown export showed **repetitive** lines; ensure live UI does not feel like duplicate **fake** rhythm — vary **copy** and show **diverse** review snippets. |
| **CTAs** | “Shop moringa”, “See products”, and blog paths are present — keep **one primary** colour and verb per section. |
| **Pain points** | Content addresses **quality vs cheap powder**, **convenience**, and **Aussie delivery** — on-brand. |
| **Trust** | Star ratings, customer quotes, and **Melbourne/Truganina** references support trust — add **verifiable** signals (e.g. “Lab testing available on request” near reviews). |

**Quick UX checks:** **Email** contact consistency (`Nutrithrive0@Gmail.com` vs lowercase) — use **one** branded address in footer. **Phone** and **form** on contact: ensure **single** clearest path to sales questions.

## Blog and content pages

| Area | Assessment |
|------|------------|
| **Usefulness** | Pillar **moringa guide**, **brand comparison**, **is moringa legit**, and **diet / how-to** are **substantive** (long, structured) — not thin. |
| **Structure** | Good use of **TOC**, **tables**, and **FAQs** on commercial guides — improves scanability. **Exception:** the **moringa brands** page is very long; consider **accordions** and **on-page** “jump to” that remain accessible. |
| **Internal links** | Cross-links to **products** and other guides are **strong** on moringa content — good for UX and SEO. **Broken pattern:** `usage-guide` path inconsistency. |
| **Next step** | Many pages end with **newsletter** and “related” — add **one** outcome-focused CTA (e.g. “Order 100g moringa to try the routine”) for readers at decision stage. |
| **Search intent** | **Gyms Melbourne**, **Darjeeling tea**, **protein** guides serve **diverse** intents — ensure each has a **one-line** “who this is for” intro so the reader doesn’t bounce when they expected only moringa. **Darjeeling** article:** remove **internal “SEO & linking strategy”** language** — it reads as **non-reader** content. |
| **Language-specific** | **Chinese moringa guide** — check **free shipping** ($30) vs site-wide **$80+** in FAQ: **inconsistency** confuses and erodes trust. |
| **Editorial** | **“Nutrites”** / similar typos in long posts — run one **pass** in Grammarly/IDE across HTML sources. **how-to-add** is **extremely** long; consider **splitting** into **two** guides (diet + recipes) with a clear hub. |

## Product and service (shop) pages

| Area | Assessment |
|------|------------|
| **One keyword / intent** | PDPs target **moringa**, **curry leaves**, **black tea**, **combo**, **soap** — **clear**. |
| **Offer in 5 seconds** | Price, what’s in the bag, and **lab/shipping** are present on moringa — good. **Combo** should repeat **savings in AUD** in hero. |
| **Objections** | **FAQ**, shipping, and comparison tables on moringa — good. **Soap** and **tea** need **same** objection depth if traffic grows. |
| **Social proof** | Reviews on PDP; **brands** article uses **narrative** reviews — keep PDP reviews **credible** (verified purchase, dates). |
| **Keyword density** | **Moringa** long copy includes **keyword-heavy** comparison blocks—read for **natural** flow; add **one** “plain English summary” box at top. |

## Contact, about, and trust pages

| Area | Assessment |
|------|------------|
| **Contact** | Form/email present — good. **Meta description** is generic/duplicated; improve with **expected reply time** and **location** (Truganina / Melbourne). |
| **About** | “Founded 2024”, team story, and mission — good **E-E-A-T** base. Add **face(s)** and **one** line on **how products are quality-checked** if not already. |
| **Address** | **Melbourne / Truganina** appears on key pages for **local** relevance — good for `LocalBusiness` alignment. **Privacy** is `noindex` on one sample: legal pages are sometimes indexed for **trust**; choose **one** policy. |
| **FAQ** | **Shipping $80+**, **returns** — make sure all **translated** and **peripheral** pages echo the same numbers. |
