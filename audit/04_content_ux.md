# Phase 4 - Content and UX Audit
Review scope: 50 pages

## Homepage
- H1 clarity: Strong - Premium Moringa Powder from Melbourne – Natural Energy with Nutri Thrive
- Value proposition above fold: Present (lab-tested, vegan, gluten-free, delivery claims in hero).
- CTA visibility: Present (Explore Products, Learn More).
- Pain points messaging: Partial (energy, bloating, immunity covered; fewer explicit customer pain scenarios).
- Trust signals: Strong (many testimonials, local address, phone number).

## Blog / Content Pages
- Helpfulness: Mixed. Many long-form comparison guides are substantive, but a subset appears tool-like/utility or thin.
- Structure: Mostly strong H2/H3 usage across major posts; some pages lack clean hierarchy.
- Internal linking: Generally good via nav/footer and in-content links, but contextual related-post linking can be improved.
- Next step clarity: Varies; product-oriented posts usually include conversion intent, informational posts less consistent.
- Search intent alignment: Strong for 'best X in Australia' and comparison terms, weaker on some broad trend pieces.

## Product / Service Pages
- Keyword/intent targeting: Strong; each product page maps to a specific item and intent.
- Offer clarity: Strong within first screen on most product pages.
- Objection handling: Medium; could add richer FAQs, comparisons, and usage outcomes per product.
- Social proof: Present site-wide, but product-page-specific reviews could be surfaced more prominently.

## Contact / About Pages
- Contact method clarity: Strong (email and phone exposed, dedicated contact page).
- About story/trust: Good foundation with brand story and local location context.
- Physical address: Present (Truganina, Melbourne) supporting local trust.
