## Duplicate & URL-hygiene notes (from map)

- **Extensionless vs `.html`:** e.g. `moringa-melbourne-complete-growers-report-2026` and `.html` — same or near-duplicate *title* in search (`Moringa Leaves Melbourne | Grower's Report 2026`); should consolidate on one URL with 301.  
- **Labs tools:** `dedup-lines` vs `dedup-lines.html` (and other tools) — same pattern.  
- **How-to moringa guide:** `pages/usage-guide/how-to-use-moringa` and `.html` both listed.  
- **Sitemap as indexable URL:** `sitemap.xml` appears in the map; usually fine as a resource, not a “page” for humans.
