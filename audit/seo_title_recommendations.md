## ADDITIONAL SEO TITLE TIPS

### Character Count Rules:
- **Optimal:** 50-60 characters (fully visible in SERPs)
- **Acceptable:** 30-50 characters
- **Avoid:** >60 chars (truncated) or <30 chars (not descriptive)

### Title Structure Best Practices:
1. **Primary Keyword First:** Put main keyword at the beginning
2. **Use Modifiers:** Add "guide," "review," "2026" for specificity
3. **Include Location:** "Australia" or "Melbourne" for local SEO
4. **Add Emotional Triggers:** "Easy," "Quick," "Best," "Complete"
5. **Year/Date:** "2026" signals freshness (update annually)

### Click-Through Rate Boosters:
- ✅ Numbers (7-day, 30-day, top 10)
- ✅ Questions (Does, Is, How, Why)
- ✅ Brackets/Parentheses (adds secondary info)
- ✅ Power words (Complete, Ultimate, Essential, Proven)
- ✅ Urgency (2026, New, Latest)
