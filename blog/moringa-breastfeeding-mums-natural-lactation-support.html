üç™ The "Boobie Ball" Recipe: No-Bake Lactation Cookies

Perfect for: Tired mums who don't have time to bake. These take 10 minutes and can be stored in the fridge for up to 2 weeks.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats (galactagogue)
  • 1/2 cup ground flaxseed (omega-3s, fiber)
  • 2 tbsp NutriThrive Moringa powder (iron, protein, B-vitamins)
  • 1/2 cup almond butter (healthy fats)
  • 1/3 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup shredded coconut (optional)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Method:

  1. Mix all dry ingredients (oats, flaxseed, Moringa, coconut, salt) in a bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine almond butter, honey, and vanilla.
  3. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and mix until well combined.
  4. Roll into 12-15 small balls (about 1 tbsp each).
  5. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before eating.
  6. Store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 2 weeks.

üí° Pro Tip: Eat 2-3 balls daily, ideally 30 minutes before feeding. The combination of oats (galactagogue), flaxseed (omega-3s), and Moringa (iron + protein) works synergistically to support milk production.

Is Moringa Safe for Breastfeeding? (Science-Backed Answer)

Short Answer: Yes, when used in moderation. Moringa has been used traditionally for centuries to support lactation, and modern research confirms its safety profile.

üìö The Research:

A 2014 study published in the Journal of Food Science and Technology found that Moringa leaves are rich in galactagogue compounds (substances that promote milk production) and are safe for consumption during breastfeeding when used in recommended amounts (1-2 teaspoons daily).

Key Findings:

  • Moringa contains phytochemicals that support prolactin production (the hormone responsible for milk supply)
  • High iron content prevents anemia, which can directly impact milk production
  • No adverse effects reported in traditional use or clinical studies when consumed in food amounts

How Galactagogues Foods Work (The Science Behind Milk Production)

Galactagogues foods are substances that help increase milk supply. They work by:

  • Stimulating Prolactin: The hormone that tells your body to produce milk. Moringa's phytochemicals support natural prolactin production.
