Why Moringa Supports Lactation

Moringa provides essential nutrients that breastfeeding mothers need:

  • Iron: Prevents anemia that can affect milk supply
  • Protein: Essential for milk production
  • B-vitamins: Support energy and metabolism
  • Calcium: Important for both mum and baby

Important: Always consult your healthcare provider before taking any supplements while breastfeeding.
