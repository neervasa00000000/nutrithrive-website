Frequently Asked Questions

How much Moringa should I take daily?

Start with 1/2 to 1 teaspoon daily, gradually increasing to 1-2 teaspoons. For capsules, take 1-2 daily with meals. Always consult your healthcare provider for personalized advice.

Is Moringa safe for everyone?

Moringa is generally safe for most people. However, pregnant women, those on blood thinners, or with specific medical conditions should consult their doctor before use.

Can I take Moringa with other supplements?

Yes, Moringa pairs well with most supplements. It's particularly beneficial when combined with vitamin C, iron, or other superfoods. Take with meals for best absorption.

How long does it take to see results?

Many people notice increased energy within 1-2 weeks. Full benefits typically develop over 4-8 weeks of consistent use. Results vary based on individual health and lifestyle.
