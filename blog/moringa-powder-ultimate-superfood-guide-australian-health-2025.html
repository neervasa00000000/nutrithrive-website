Moringa Side Effects You Should Know

While moringa is generally safe, it's important to be aware of potential side effects:

‚ö†Ô∏è Potential Side Effects:

  • Digestive Issues: May cause mild stomach upset, diarrhea, or nausea in some people (start with small doses)
  • Blood Thinning: Contains compounds that may thin blood - avoid if taking blood thinners
  • Pregnancy Concerns: May cause uterine contractions - avoid during pregnancy
  • Hypoglycemia: Can lower blood sugar - monitor if diabetic
  • Interactions: May interact with certain medications (consult your doctor)

Safety Tips: Start with small doses (¬Ω teaspoon), stay within recommended limits (6 teaspoons max), and consult your healthcare provider if you have medical conditions or take medications.
