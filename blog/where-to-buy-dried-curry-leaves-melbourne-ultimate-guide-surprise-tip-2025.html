Are curry leaves the same as curry powder?

No. Curry leaves come from the Murraya koenigii tree; curry powder is a spice blend.

Can I substitute dried for fresh?

Yes — use 2–3× more dried and temper early in hot oil.

How do I store dried curry leaves in Melbourne’s humid climate?

Store in an airtight glass jar in a cool, dark cupboard. Avoid the fridge.
