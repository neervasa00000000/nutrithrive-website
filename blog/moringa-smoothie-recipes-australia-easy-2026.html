Moringa Smoothie Recipes Australia: Easy 2026 Guide

25 June 2026 · 6 min read · By Neer Vasa

Smoothies are the easiest way to use moringa powder without tasting lawn clippings. If you dose correctly and blend long enough. These three recipes use ingredients you can find at Woolworths or Coles.

How much moringa per smoothie?

ExperiencePowderApprox. weight
New to moringa½ tsp~1–1.5 g
Regular use1 tsp~2–3 g
Do not exceed without GP advice2+ tspSee side effects guide

Clump-free blending

Whisk powder with 1 tbsp warm water into a paste before adding cold fruit. Blend 45–60 seconds. Fat helps mouthfeel: yoghurt, nut butter, or avocado.

Recipe 1: Tropical green (beginner)

  • ½ tsp moringa powder
  • 1 frozen banana
  • ½ cup frozen mango
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or oat)
  • 1 tbsp Greek yoghurt

Recipe 2: Berry cacao (hides grassiness)

  • ½–1 tsp moringa powder
  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1 tbsp cacao powder
  • 1 cup milk + 1 tsp honey or maple

Recipe 3: Green peanut satay (savoury-sweet)

  • ½ tsp moringa powder
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • Pinch cinnamon

FAQ

How much moringa powder in a smoothie?
Start with ½ teaspoon per serve; increase slowly if your stomach tolerates it.
How do I stop moringa clumping?
Paste with warm water first, then blend with fruit and liquid at high speed.
Best time for a moringa smoothie?
Morning with breakfast is easiest to repeat daily: consistency beats perfect timing.
