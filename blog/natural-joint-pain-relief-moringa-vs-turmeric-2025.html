Moringa vs. Turmeric: The Anti-Inflammatory Showdown

Both Moringa and Turmeric contain powerful anti-inflammatory compounds, but they work differently:

  • Moringa: Contains isothiocyanates and quercetin that reduce inflammation
  • Turmeric: Contains curcumin, the active compound that fights inflammation

Which Works Better?

Research shows both can help, but Moringa offers additional benefits like complete protein for joint health and higher bioavailability when taken with meals.
