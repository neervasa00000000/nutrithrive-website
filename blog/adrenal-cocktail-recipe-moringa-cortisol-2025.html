The classic recipe you see online usually looks like this: Orange Juice (Vitamin C) + Coconut Water (Potassium) + Sea Salt (Sodium).

While this works, it contains a huge spike of fructose (sugar) from the orange juice. For many people, this blood sugar spike can actually lead to more fatigue an hour later.

The NutriThrive "Adrenal Rescue" Recipe

We've swapped the high-sugar juice for NutriThrive Moringa, which provides the Potassium and Vitamin C you need, plus Magnesium to physically relax your muscles‚Äîall without the sugar crash.

Adrenal Rescue Mocktail

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup Coconut Water (Nature's best source of Potassium)
  • 1/2 cup Sparkling Mineral Water (For hydration and fizz)
  • 1/2 tsp NutriThrive Moringa Powder (Packed with Vitamin C, Magnesium & B-Vitamins)
  • 1/4 tsp Sea Salt or Celtic Salt (Essential Sodium for adrenal function)
  • Squeeze of fresh Lemon or Lime (For flavour and extra Vitamin C)
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Add the Moringa powder and a splash of coconut water to a glass. Use a frother or spoon to mix it into a smooth paste (this prevents clumps!).
  2. Pour in the rest of the coconut water and squeeze in your lemon juice.
  3. Add the salt and stir well.
  4. Top with sparkling water and plenty of ice.
  5. Optional: Garnish with a slice of cucumber or mint for that "spa day" feeling.
