☕ How to Brew Perfect Black Tea in Melbourne

🌡️ Water Temperature

Use freshly boiled water (100°C) for optimal extraction of flavors and health compounds.

⏰ Steeping Time

Steep for 3-5 minutes for balanced flavor. Longer steeping increases antioxidant content but may create bitterness.

🍯 Serving Suggestions

Enjoy black tea with a splash of milk, honey, or lemon. Each addition offers unique health benefits and flavor profiles.

❓ FAQ

How many cups of black tea should I drink daily?

3-4 cups daily is optimal for health benefits. Avoid excessive consumption to prevent caffeine sensitivity.

Is black tea better than green tea?

Both offer unique benefits. Black tea has more caffeine and different antioxidants, while green tea has higher EGCG content.

Can black tea help with weight loss?

Black tea can support metabolism and provide a low-calorie alternative to sugary drinks, but it's not a magic weight loss solution.
