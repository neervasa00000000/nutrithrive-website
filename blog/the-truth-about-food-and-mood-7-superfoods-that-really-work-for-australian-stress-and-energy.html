FAQ

Do mood superfoods really work?

Yes, when chosen correctly. Foods rich in magnesium, B vitamins, antioxidants, and probiotics have been shown in scientific studies to support mood regulation, reduce stress-related fatigue, and improve mental clarity. The key is consistency and choosing whole, nutrient-dense foods over processed alternatives.

What's the best food for anxiety and daily stress?

Moringa powder is excellent for anxiety and stress because it's packed with magnesium (which helps calm the nervous system) and vitamin B6 (essential for serotonin production). Black tea with L-theanine also provides calm, focused energy without the jitters. Pumpkin seeds are another great option due to their tryptophan and magnesium content.

What to eat for focus and calm energy?

Black tea is ideal for focus and calm energy because L-theanine works synergistically with caffeine to provide alertness without anxiety. Moringa can also support sustained energy levels. Berries provide antioxidants that protect brain cells, while curry leaves support gut health, which is directly linked to cognitive function.

Can gut health improve mood and sleep?

Absolutely. The gut-brain axis is well-documented in scientific literature. Probiotics from fermented foods like kimchi and sauerkraut help produce serotonin (about 90% of serotonin is made in the gut). A healthy gut microbiome also supports better sleep quality, which in turn improves mood and energy levels throughout the day.
