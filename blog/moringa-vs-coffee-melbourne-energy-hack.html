The Moringa Latte: Your Coffee Replacement Recipe

One of the biggest challenges when switching from coffee is missing the ritual. The morning coffee routine is comforting. That's why we've created a Moringa Latte recipe that gives you that same satisfying experienceâ€”without the caffeine crash.

The "Executive Energy" Moringa Latte:

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon NutriThrive Moringa Powder
  • 1 cup steamed oat milk (or your preferred milk alternative)
  • 1 teaspoon honey or maple syrup (optional, for sweetness)
  • A dash of cinnamon (for that cozy, comforting feel)
  • A pinch of vanilla extract (optional)

Method:

  1. Whisk the Moringa powder with a small splash of hot water until smooth (this prevents clumping)
  2. Heat your milk of choice until steaming (not boiling)
  3. Froth the milk if you have a frother, or simply heat it
  4. Pour the Moringa mixture into your cup
  5. Add honey/maple syrup and vanilla if using
  6. Pour the steamed milk over the Moringa mixture
  7. Sprinkle with cinnamon
  8. Enjoy your caffeine-free energy boost!

Time: Takes just 2-3 minutesâ€”perfect for a quick office break or morning routine.

Tip: The key to a smooth Moringa latte is mixing the powder with a small amount of hot water first. This prevents clumping and ensures even distribution.
