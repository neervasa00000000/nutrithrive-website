Forget the 10-Step Routine: Why "Edible Skincare" is the Viral Trend of Summer 2025

Discover how internal skincare with NutriThrive Moringa gives you that 'lit from within' glow

Home › Blog › Edible Skincare

If you walked into a dermatologist's office in 2024, the advice was all about what you put on your face: Retinol, Vitamin C serums, and endless moisturisers.

But in late 2025, the conversation in Australia has shifted.

The biggest beauty trend right now isn't a new cream—it's "Edible Beauty."

From TikTok feeds to wellness clinics in Bondi and Melbourne, the focus has moved to internal skincare—giving your body the raw materials it needs to build collagen, fight UV damage, and glow from the inside out.

And the hero ingredient leading this charge? It's not collagen powder (which many find hard to digest). It's the plant-based powerhouse: Moringa.

Here is why your expensive creams might not be working, and why a daily scoop of greens is the summer skincare hack you need.

The "Internal Retinol" Connection

You've likely heard of Retinol. It's the gold standard for anti-ageing because it speeds up cell turnover. But synthetic retinol can be harsh, causing redness and peeling—especially in the harsh Australian sun.

Here is the secret: Vitamin A is the natural precursor to retinol. Your body uses Vitamin A to repair skin tissue and keep it firm.

Moringa leaves contain roughly 10x more Vitamin A than carrots.

When you drink NutriThrive Moringa, you are essentially consuming a gentle, natural "internal retinol." You are flooding your system with the exact nutrient your skin needs to repair itself, without the harsh side effects of topical acids.

Why You Need "Sun Food" This Summer

As we head into a hot Australian summer, UV damage is the enemy. UV rays cause "oxidative stress" (rusting of the cells), which leads to fine lines and pigmentation.

You can wear sunscreen (and you should!), but sunscreen only protects the surface.

To protect the deeper layers of your skin, you need Antioxidants. Moringa has one of the highest antioxidant scores (ORAC value) of any food on earth—higher than acai, matcha, or blueberries.

Think of it as an internal shield. While sunscreen blocks the rays on the outside, the antioxidants in NutriThrive Moringa help neutralise the free radicals that manage to get through.

The "Gut-Skin" Axis (Goodbye, Breakouts)

Have you ever noticed that when you eat sugar or greasy food, your skin breaks out? That's the Gut-Skin Axis.

If your gut is inflamed or sluggish, toxins recirculate in your body and often try to escape through your skin (hello, acne and eczema).

Topical creams can't fix a gut issue. Moringa can.

  • It's a natural detoxifier: It supports liver function, helping your body filter out toxins efficiently.
  • It's anti-inflammatory: It calms the internal inflammation that causes redness and puffiness.
  • It's high in fibre: A healthy gut microbiome equals clearer, brighter skin.

For additional digestive support, consider pairing NutriThrive Moringa with our premium curry leaves, which are also known for their gut health benefits.

How to "Drink Your Skincare"

You don't need to overhaul your life to jump on the Edible Beauty trend. You just need to add one step to your morning.

The "Summer Glow" Shot:

  • 1/2 tsp NutriThrive Moringa
  • Juice of 1/2 Lemon (Vitamin C boosts collagen production)
  • 1 cup Water or Coconut Water

Drink this first thing in the morning on an empty stomach. The combination of Vitamin A (from Moringa) and Vitamin C (from Lemon) is the ultimate recipe for collagen synthesis.

For an extra boost, you can also enjoy our premium black tea later in the day, which contains additional antioxidants that support skin health.

The Verdict

Skincare creams are great, but they can only go so deep. If you want that "lit from within" look that is trending this summer, you have to actually start from within.
