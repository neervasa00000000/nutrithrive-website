How to Build a Whole Food Plant-Based Diet (The Beginner's Guide)

For a plant based diet Australia for beginners, focus on whole foods over processed products. Here's how to structure your meals:

The Whole Food Plant-Based Plate:

  • Protein (25% of plate): Moringa powder (1-2 tsp), hemp seeds (3 tbsp), lentils (1 cup cooked), or wattleseed flour
  • Complex Carbs (25% of plate): Brown rice, quinoa, sweet potato, or whole grain bread
  • Vegetables (50% of plate): Leafy greens, broccoli, bell peppers, carrots
  • Healthy Fats: Avocado, hemp seeds, nuts, or olive oil
  • Superfood Boost: Add Kakadu Plum powder (1/4 tsp) for Vitamin C, or Moringa for complete protein
