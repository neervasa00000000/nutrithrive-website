Weight and Gut Health: What Finally Shifted for Me

Home › Blog › Gut Health & Weight Loss

I Tried Everything. Nothing Worked.

Look, I'm just gonna say it upfront: I tried to lose weight for two straight years and failed spectacularly every single time.

Keto (twice). Intermittent fasting. Calorie counting until I wanted to throw my phone. Ran 5k three times a week. Gym at 5:30am like a psychopath.

I'd lose 2–3 kilos, feel amazing for eleven days, then gain it all back plus extra. Every. Single. Time.

My doctor said "eat less, move more." I was sitting there thinking — yeah mate, never occurred to me.

At 2am on Reddit I found a thread about gut health and weight loss. Bloating. Constant hunger after eating. 3pm crashes. Unexplainable weight gain. That was me.

I rolled my eyes at "gut health" — until I learned I'd been so focused on calories and cardio that I missed what was actually making me fat.

My gut felt wrecked. After simple changes plus moringa as a food supplement from Truganina, my weight shifted noticeably over several months — I didn't count every kilo obsessively. I ate more whole food. I exercised less hard. Results vary; see your GP for unexplained weight gain or loss.

The Actual Problem: My Gut Was Destroyed

Trillions of gut bacteria — when good outnumber bad, you digest properly, feel full, burn fat, hormones behave. When bad bacteria win, your body fights weight loss.

  1. Bad bacteria crave sugar and carbs
  2. Inflammation → store fat instead of burn
  3. Hunger hormones (leptin/ghrelin) break
  4. Metabolism slows; you're exhausted → move less

My "healthy" dieting made it worse: antibiotics, diet drinks, low-fat everything, stress, not enough fiber.

The Reddit Thread That Changed Everything

"If you're bloated all the time, exhausted after eating, craving sugar constantly, and can't lose weight even when you're doing everything right… it's your gut. Fix that first."

I had every symptom. Medical studies on microbiome and obesity — inflammation, calorie extraction, hunger hormones. At 4:30am I thought: what if this is real?

What I Actually Did (3 Phases)

Phase 1: Kill bad bacteria (Weeks 1–4)

  • Oregano oil + berberine; zero sugar
  • Eggs/avocado/spinach; chicken/broccoli; salmon/cauliflower rice
  • No bread, fruit, dairy, alcohol

Weeks 1–2: headaches, irritable (die-off). Week 3–4: less bloating, actually full after meals. Scales down about 4kg for me.

Phase 3: Maintenance (Week 13+)

Daily probiotic + moringa + omega-3. 80/20 eating. Walk 20–30 min most days — no gym. I kept losing slowly with less intense exercise — your results may differ.

Why Gut Healing Actually Works

Good bacteria: digest properly, produce fat-burning short-chain fatty acids, regulate hunger, extract fewer calories.

Bad bacteria: inflammation, sugar cravings, broken leptin/ghrelin, extract more calories from the same meal.

Leaky gut → inflammation → "protect mode" → store fat even at 1,200 calories. Fix inflammation → body burns again.
