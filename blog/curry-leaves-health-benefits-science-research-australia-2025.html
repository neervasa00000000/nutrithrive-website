How to Make Curry Leaf Tea (Benefits + Recipe)

Curry leaf tea is an easy and delicious way to enjoy curry leaves' health benefits daily.

‚òï Curry Leaf Tea Recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 15-20 dried curry leaves (or 8-10 fresh)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)
  • 1 tsp lemon juice (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp ginger (optional)

Method:

  1. Boil 2 cups of water
  2. Add curry leaves and ginger (if using)
  3. Simmer for 5-7 minutes
  4. Strain the tea
  5. Add honey and lemon to taste
  6. Drink warm, preferably on empty stomach
