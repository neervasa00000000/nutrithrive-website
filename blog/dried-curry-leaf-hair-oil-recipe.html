The 3-Ingredient DIY Recipe

Stop spending $50 on "Growth Serums." Make this for under $5 using NutriThrive dried curry leaves.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Cup Coconut Oil (Virgin is best)
  • 1/2 Cup NutriThrive Dried Curry Leaves
  • 1 tsp Fenugreek Seeds (Optional, but adds extra hair strengthening benefits)

Method:

  1. Pour the coconut oil into a small pan on very low heat (don't let it smoke).
  2. Crush the dried curry leaves in your palm to release the essential oils.
  3. Add the leaves (and fenugreek if using) to the warm oil.
  4. Simmer for 5-8 minutes until the oil turns a dark green/brown. Do not burn it - keep the heat very low.
  5. Remove from heat and let it cool completely.
  6. Strain the oil through a fine mesh or cheesecloth into a clean glass bottle.
  7. Store in a cool, dark place. The oil will last for 3-6 months.
