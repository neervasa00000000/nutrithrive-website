How much moringa to “close the gap”?

A 50 kg vegan might target ~40 g protein daily from food; a 70 kg athlete might aim much higher. One teaspoon of moringa might add ~1 g protein — useful, not a meal replacement. Stack moringa with lentils, soy, quinoa, and occasional isolates. Compare algae vs leaf in our moringa vs spirulina vs matcha explainer.

Why Vegans Need Complete Protein

Complete proteins contain all 9 essential amino acids. Most plant foods are incomplete, requiring careful food combining. Moringa leaf powder helps because it carries the full EAA spectrum in a food-matrix format — still pair it with legumes and grains.
