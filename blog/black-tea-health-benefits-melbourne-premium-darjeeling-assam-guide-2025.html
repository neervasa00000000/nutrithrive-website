FAQ

How much black tea should I drink daily?

2-4 cups daily is ideal for health benefits. Start with 1-2 cups and gradually increase. Listen to your body and adjust based on caffeine sensitivity.

Is black tea better than green tea?

Both offer unique benefits. Black tea has more caffeine and different antioxidants. Green tea has more EGCG. Many people enjoy both for variety.

Can I drink black tea on an empty stomach?

Yes, but some people find it too strong. If you're sensitive, try with a light snack or add milk to reduce tannins.

What's the best time to drink black tea in Melbourne?

Morning for energy, afternoon for focus, evening for relaxation (decaf). Melbourne's cool climate makes black tea perfect year-round.
