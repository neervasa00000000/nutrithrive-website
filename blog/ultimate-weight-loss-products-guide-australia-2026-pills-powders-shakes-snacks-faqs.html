Moringa leaves are rich in vitamin C, vitamin A precursors, calcium, iron, and plant antioxidants, which may support overall nutrition, blood sugar and cholesterol control, and anti-inflammatory activity in people with poor diets. Leaf powder contains protein, fiber, vitamins A, C, E, B vitamins, calcium, potassium, iron, and phytochemicals like flavonoids and glucosinolates; content varies by source and processing. Tea made from leaves mainly provides some antioxidants and small amounts of micronutrients, not full meal-level nutrition.
