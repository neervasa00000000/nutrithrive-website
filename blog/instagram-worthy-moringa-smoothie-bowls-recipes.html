Recipe 1: Tropical Green Goddess Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1 cup frozen mango chunks
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon Moringa powder
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1/2 cup spinach (for extra green color)

Toppings:

  • Sliced kiwi
  • Fresh mango chunks
  • Coconut flakes
  • Pomegranate seeds
  • Edible flowers (optional)

Instructions:

Blend all base ingredients until smooth and thick. Pour into a bowl and arrange toppings in an artistic pattern. The vibrant green base with colorful tropical fruits creates a stunning visual contrast.

Recipe 2: Berry Green Power Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon Moringa powder
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • 1/2 cup spinach

Toppings:

  • Fresh berries
  • Granola clusters
  • Sliced almonds
  • Hemp seeds
  • Drizzle of honey or maple syrup

Instructions:

Blend until smooth and creamy. The combination of berries and Moringa creates a beautiful deep purple-green color. Top with contrasting textures for visual appeal.

Recipe 3: Chocolate Green Dream Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 avocado (for creaminess)
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon Moringa powder
  • 2 tablespoons cacao powder
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup spinach

Toppings:

  • Cacao nibs
  • Fresh raspberries
  • Goji berries
  • Shredded coconut
  • Pumpkin seeds

Instructions:

Blend until smooth. The cacao and Moringa create a rich, earthy green-brown color that's both beautiful and delicious. Perfect for chocolate lovers who want their greens too.

Tips for Instagram-Worthy Presentation

  • Use a wide, shallow bowl: This gives you more surface area for artistic toppings
  • Create sections: Arrange toppings in distinct sections rather than mixing them all together
  • Add height: Pile some toppings higher in the center for dimension
  • Use contrasting colors: Bright fruits against the green base create visual interest
  • Natural lighting: Photograph near a window for the best natural light
  • Garnish with herbs: Fresh mint leaves or edible flowers add a professional touch
