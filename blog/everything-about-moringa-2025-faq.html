Top 20 Moringa Questions Answered

1. What does Moringa taste like?

Mild, earthy, slightly grassyâ€”similar to matcha or green tea. Much milder than spirulina.

2. Can I put Moringa in hot coffee?

Yes, but warm is better. Very high heat can degrade some nutrients. Let your coffee cool slightly before adding Moringa.

3. How do I store Moringa powder?

Keep it in a cool, dry place away from light. An airtight container in your pantry is perfect. It will stay fresh for up to 2 years.

4. Does Moringa expire?

Yes, but it has a long shelf lifeâ€”typically 2 years when stored properly. Check the expiration date on your package.

5. Can I cook with Moringa?

Absolutely! Add it to soups, sauces, smoothies, baked goods, and more. Just avoid very high heat to preserve nutrients.

6. How much Moringa should I take daily?

Start with 1 teaspoon per day. You can increase to 1-2 tablespoons based on your needs and tolerance.

7. When is the best time to take Moringa?

Morning is popular for energy, but you can take it anytime. Some prefer it with meals to help with nutrient absorption.

8. Can kids take Moringa?

Yes, in smaller amounts. Start with 1/4 teaspoon for children. It's great for hiding in their food!

9. Is Moringa safe during pregnancy?

Consult your doctor first. While Moringa is generally safe, pregnancy requires extra caution with any supplement.

10. Does Moringa have caffeine?

No, Moringa is caffeine-free. It provides natural energy without the jitters or crash.

11. Can I take Moringa on an empty stomach?

Yes, many people do. If you experience any stomach sensitivity, try taking it with food.

12. Will Moringa make me gain weight?

No, Moringa is low in calories and can actually support weight management through appetite control and metabolism support.

13. How is Moringa different from matcha?

Moringa has more protein, iron, and Vitamin A. Matcha has caffeine; Moringa doesn't. Both are green superfoods with different benefits.

14. Can I mix Moringa with protein powder?

Yes! Moringa pairs well with protein powders and can boost the nutritional profile of your shake.

15. Does Moringa help with sleep?

Some people find it helps with sleep quality due to its magnesium content and adaptogenic properties, but it's not a sedative.

16. Is Moringa organic?

Look for certified organic Moringa. Our NutriThrive Moringa is 100% organic and lab-tested.

17. Can I use Moringa in baking?

Yes! Add it to muffins, pancakes, bread, and cookies. It adds nutrients without significantly changing the taste.

18. Does Moringa interact with medications?

Moringa may interact with blood sugar and blood pressure medications. Always consult your doctor if you're on medication.

19. How do I know if my Moringa is high quality?

20. Can I take Moringa every day?

Yes! Daily use is recommended for best results. Consistency is key to experiencing the full benefits.
