What Is Moringa Powder?

Moringa oleifera is often called the "miracle tree" because its leaves contain a dense combination of vitamins, minerals, plant compounds, and protein. These leaves have been used for generations in traditional food and wellness routines across India and parts of Africa.

To produce moringa powder, leaves are harvested, dried, and finely ground. The resulting green powder can be mixed into drinks and meals, which makes it one of the most practical plant-based supplements for daily use.

Nutrients commonly associated with moringa include vitamin A, vitamin C, iron, calcium, potassium, plant protein, and antioxidant polyphenols.
