What Exactly Is Moringa?

Moringa oleifera has been used for centuries across India, Africa, and Southeast Asia. Today, the most common form is moringa leaf powder, made by drying and grinding the leaves into a fine green powder.

People add it to smoothies, teas, and meals to increase plant-based nutrition in a practical daily format.

Researchers often highlight moringa for its broad nutrient profile, including naturally occurring nutrients, antioxidant compounds, and all nine essential amino acids.

How to Use Moringa Powder (Simple Daily Ideas)
