What is a Galactagogue?

A galactagogue is a substance that helps increase milk production in breastfeeding mothers. Moringa has been used as a natural galactagogue for centuries in traditional medicine, particularly in the Philippines and India, where it's known as "malunggay."

Research suggests that Moringa may help increase milk supply by:

  • Supporting prolactin production (the hormone responsible for milk production)
  • Providing essential nutrients needed for milk production
  • Improving overall maternal health and energy levels

Is Moringa Safe for Breastfeeding?

Moringa has been used safely by breastfeeding mothers for centuries in traditional medicine. However, it's always important to:

  • Consult your doctor or lactation consultant before starting any new supplement while breastfeeding
  • Start with a small dose (1 teaspoon daily) and monitor your baby for any reactions
  • Choose high-quality, 100% natural Moringa like NutriThrive to ensure purity
  • Monitor your milk supply and your baby's feeding patterns

Important: While Moringa is generally considered safe, always consult with your healthcare provider before taking any supplement while breastfeeding, especially if you have any medical conditions or are taking medications.
