The Myth: "Dried Leaves Have No Flavor"

If you throw dried curry leaves directly into a simmering curry, they will taste like paper. This is why they get a bad reputation.

Unlike fresh leaves, dried leaves need to be woken up. The essential oils are dormant, locked inside the dehydrated leaf. You have to unlock them.

The Secret Trick: The "Hot Oil" Infusion

To make dried curry leaves taste 90% as good as fresh, you must follow this one rule:

Always fry them in hot fat (Tadka).

  1. Heat your ghee, coconut oil, or vegetable oil until shimmering.
  2. Crush the dried leaves slightly in your hand to break the cell walls.
  3. Throw them into the hot oil before you add onions or garlic.
  4. Wait 10 seconds. You will smell a sudden burst of citrus and nuttiness. That is the flavor releasing.
