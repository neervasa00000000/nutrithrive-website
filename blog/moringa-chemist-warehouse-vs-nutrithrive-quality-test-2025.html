How to Check Chemist Warehouse Moringa Quality in 2 Minutes

Whether you already have a bottle or are considering buying one, these checks take under two minutes:

  1. Open a capsule: Break one open and look at the powder. Vibrant green = relatively fresh. Brown or dull olive = oxidized. Rosabella's clear bottle gives UV light direct access to the powder from the moment it hits the shelf.
  2. Smell it: Fresh moringa smells earthy and slightly grassy. Musty, flat, or stale smells mean degradation has already started.
