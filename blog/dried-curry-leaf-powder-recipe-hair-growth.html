The "Green Gold" Recipe (Takes 2 Minutes)

This "Gunpowder" sprinkle tastes amazing on avocado toast, eggs, rice, or even mixed into yogurt. It's a versatile condiment that packs a nutritional punch.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Cup NutriThrive Dried Curry Leaves (You need a whole bag!)
  • 2 tbsp Sesame Seeds (Calcium boost for hair strength)
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper (Helps with nutrient absorption)
  • Salt to taste (optional, but enhances flavor)

Method:

  1. Dry roast the sesame seeds and black pepper in a pan over low heat for 1 minute until fragrant. Be careful not to burn them.
  2. Add the dried curry leaves for just 10 seconds to warm them up and release their essential oils. Don't overheat or they'll lose their nutrients.
  3. Let everything cool completely before grinding (this prevents the powder from clumping).
  4. Blitz everything in a blender or spice grinder until you get a fine powder. You may need to pulse it a few times to get the right consistency.
  5. Store in an airtight jar in a cool, dark place. The powder will last for 3-6 months.

üí° How to Use: Eat 1 teaspoon daily. Sprinkle it on rice, mix it into eggs, add it to avocado toast, or stir it into yogurt. The roasted sesame and pepper make it taste like a savoury spice mix, not a leaf.
