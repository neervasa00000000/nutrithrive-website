What Makes a Superfood "Super"?

Superfoods are nutrient-dense foods that provide exceptional health benefits beyond basic nutrition. They're packed with antioxidants, vitamins, minerals, and bioactive compounds that support optimal health and wellness.

Key Characteristics of Superfoods:

  • High nutrient density: More nutrients per calorie than regular foods
  • Antioxidant-rich: Combat oxidative stress and inflammation
  • Bioactive compounds: Unique plant compounds with health benefits
  • Minimal processing: Natural, whole food sources
