What to Look For When Buying Moringa

Regardless of where you buy, here's what to check:

  • Price per 100g: Compare prices—anything over $15 per 100g is expensive

How to test if your moringa is still fresh (30-second test)

The colour test: Hold your moringa powder up to the light. Fresh moringa looks vibrant green; old or degraded powder shifts olive, brown, or grey.
