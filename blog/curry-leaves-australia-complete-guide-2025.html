🍵 3. How to Make Curry Leaf Tea (Simple Recipe)

Curry leaf tea is becoming a health trend in Australia for its detoxifying and digestive properties.

Ingredients:

  • 10–12 dried curry leaves (or 5–6 fresh)
  • 1 cup water
  • Optional: lemon juice or honey

Method:

  1. Boil 1 cup water.
  2. Add curry leaves and simmer 5–7 minutes.
  3. Strain and drink warm.

✅ Great for metabolism, hair health, and calming your stomach after meals.

🥘 4. How to Use Curry Leaves in Everyday Cooking

🔥 Tempering (Tadka)

Heat oil/ghee, add curry leaves, and use to flavour dals, chutneys, and curries.

🌶️ Spice Powders

Add to spice blends like Karuveppilai Podi for rice dishes.

🍝 Fusion Recipes

Fry in butter to create a flavour base for roast vegetables or pasta.

🍲 Soups & Stews

Crumble over soups and stews for a unique citrusy note.

💆 Hair Tonic

Infuse in coconut oil for a traditional hair treatment.

📦 6. Storage Tips for Maximum Freshness

🫙

Store dried leaves in a sealed glass jar in a cool, dark place.

🌡️

Avoid refrigeration — room temperature preserves aroma better.

🥄

Always use a dry spoon to prevent moisture.

⏰

Stored correctly, dried leaves can last 6–12 months.
