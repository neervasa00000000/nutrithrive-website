Is Moringa Safe for Dogs?

Yes, Moringa is generally safe for dogs when given in appropriate amounts. Start with small doses (1/4 teaspoon for small dogs, 1/2 teaspoon for larger dogs) mixed into their food.

Benefits for Dogs

  • Healthy coat: Omega-3s and vitamins support shiny, healthy fur
  • Joint health: Anti-inflammatory properties help with arthritis
  • Immune support: Antioxidants boost their natural defenses
  • Digestive health: Fiber supports healthy digestion

Important: Always consult your veterinarian before adding supplements to your dog's diet.
