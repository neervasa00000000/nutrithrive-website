How to Make Curry Leaf Tea

Curry leaf tea is a simple and aromatic way to enjoy the flavor of curry leaves. Here's how to prepare it:

Curry Leaf Tea Recipe

  1. Crush the leaves: Take 10-15 dried curry leaves and gently crush them in your palm to release the essential oils.
  2. Boil water: Bring 2 cups of water to a rolling boil.
  3. Steep: Add the crushed leaves to the boiling water and let it steep for 5-7 minutes on low heat.
  4. Strain and serve: Strain the tea and add a pinch of salt or honey to taste. Drink warm on an empty stomach for best results.

Tip: Dried leaves steep better than fresh because the cell walls are already broken, allowing the flavor compounds to release more easily.
