The Athlete's Protocol: Timing Moringa for Peak Performance & Sleep Recovery

Published November 2025

Timing is everything. You wouldn’t drink a double espresso before bed, and you wouldn’t take melatonin before a deadlift PR. Treat moringa like a performance supplement, not a garnish. Here’s the 3-phase protocol built with Australian coaches to maximise output and minimise recovery time.

Phase 1: Pre-Workout (The "Clean Energy" Window)

  • Timing: 30–45 minutes before training
  • Dosage: 5g (1 teaspoon)
  • Mix: Water + pinch sea salt + lemon
  • Why: Floods electrolytes and B-vitamins before sweating. Iron and magnesium give calm, steady energy—no caffeine spike or crash.

Phase 2: Post-Workout (The Inflammation Window)

  • Timing: Within 1 hour of finishing
  • Dosage: 5g (1 teaspoon) in your protein shake
  • Why: Isothiocyanates hit the inflammatory markers that drive DOMS. Pairs perfectly with vanilla or berry protein.

Phase 3: Rest Days (The Recovery Window)

  • Timing: First thing in the morning
  • Dosage: 5g in a green smoothie
  • Why: Rest days are when you grow. A steady antioxidant stream keeps joints and ligaments repairing before the next heavy session.

Can I Take It Before Bed?

Yes. Zero caffeine. The magnesium promotes muscle relaxation and deeper REM sleep (when HGH is released).

Sleep stack: 1/2 tsp moringa + warm almond milk + honey, 30 minutes before sleep.
