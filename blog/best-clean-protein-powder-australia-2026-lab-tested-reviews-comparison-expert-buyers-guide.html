How to Choose the Best Clean Protein for You

For Sensitive Stomachs

Opt for single-ingredient or simple blends (pea, pumpkin, moringa). These are easier to digest and less likely to cause bloating or digestive discomfort.

For Muscle Recovery

Seek higher leucine content (check product nutrition labels). Leucine is a key amino acid for muscle protein synthesis and recovery after workouts.

For Everyday Wellness

Pick a blend that includes greens (moringa, spirulina, hemp). These provide additional vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants beyond just protein.
