Select Your Ritual

The Melbourne Royal

Stovetop Simmer Method

2 Min Prep 6 Min Cook

Composition

  • 2 heaping tsp Nutri Thrive Premium Black Tea
  • 150ml Filtered Water
  • 150ml Milk (Bonsoy or Full Cream)
  • 1-2 tsp Brown Sugar (for depth)

Methodology

  • The Bloom

    Bring water to a boil in a copper or steel saucepan. Add tea leaves. Simmer gently for 2 minutes until the liquor is a deep crimson.

  • The Cream

    Pour in the milk. Critical: Heat gently until steam rises (~80Â°C). Do not boil rapidly, lest the proteins separate.

  • The Steep

    Remove from heat, cover, and rest for 3 minutes. This allows the tannins to marry with the milk proteins.

The Hong Kong Silk

The Potent Brew

1 Min Prep 8 Min Cook

Composition

  • 1 tbsp Nutri Thrive Premium Black Tea
  • 200ml Boiling Water
  • 2 tbsp Evaporated Milk
  • 1 tsp Condensed Milk

Methodology

  • The Concentrate

    Create a potent base. Steep leaves in boiling water for 5-8 minutes.

  • The Pull

    Optional: Pour tea back and forth between two vessels to aerate and smooth the tannins.

  • The Union

    Place milks in your chalice. Pour the hot tea concentrate over them and stir vigorously.

The Brisbane Chill

Iced Royal Method

5 Min Prep 4 Min Cook

Composition

  • 2 tsp Nutri Thrive Premium Black Tea
  • 150ml Boiling Water
  • Ice Cubes
  • 100ml Cold Oat Milk

Methodology

  • The Infusion

    Steep tea in boiling water for exactly 4 minutes.

  • The Shock

    Strain into a shaker. Add sugar immediately. Add ice and shake until frost forms on the metal.

  • The Service

    Pour into a tall glass over fresh ice. Crown with cold milk.
