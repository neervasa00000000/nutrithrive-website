Flagged issues and human fixes

Stricter checklist: AI verbs, inflated adjectives, filler nouns, passive openings, predictable transitions, flat 15–20 word rhythm, formulaic intro/outro, AU EEAT gaps, and hype tone. Each item includes a rewrite direction (not auto-edited text).

Forensic checklist (strict thresholds)

Tick-style audit: em-dash density, “The” clustering in a paragraph, parallel twin adjectives, passive share, cheerleading, H2 “X for Y” symmetry, consecutive starters, trigram echo, softeners, clichés. If more than ~3 rows fire, treat the draft as high-risk for AI-shaped prose.
